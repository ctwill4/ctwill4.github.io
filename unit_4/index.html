Sprokets Inc.

Feel the Power of Sprockets!

Ever since I was a young child, I knew sprockets would be the way of the future. For well over a century, man has been fascinated with the creation of machines. Today, they are more sophisticated than ever, requiring an untold amount of parts to perform the most simple of tasks. Sprokets will help reduce the amount of required parts for machines of all sizes and complex builds.

The following is a list of items sprokets can be used in:

  • Boats
  • Mechanical Bulls
  • Smaller Sprokets
  • Washing Machines
  • Larger Sprockets
  • Helicopters
  • Gundam Wings

And these are NOT the sprokets we are discussing
